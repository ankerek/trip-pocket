Design a landing page for Trip Pocket — a mobile app.

**Product**
- **Name:** Trip Pocket
- **Tagline:** Your pocket for travel ideas.
- **One-liner:** A dedicated inbox for the travel screenshots you save from Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube — grouped by trip, ready when you go.
- **Platform:** iOS (App Store launch). Android coming later.
- **Handle / domain:** @trippocket.app / trippocket.app

**Audience & problem**
Travelers who screenshot social-media inspiration but lose it in their camera roll. They don't want a trip planner — they want a calm place to save, sort, and rediscover ideas before a trip.

**Sections (in order)**
1. **Hero** — product name, tagline, one-sentence subhead, primary CTA "Download on the App Store" (use the official Apple badge), secondary link "See how it works." Right side: a clean iPhone mockup showing the app's inbox view (grid of travel screenshots).
2. **The problem** — short, empathetic line: "Your screenshots folder is a graveyard of trip ideas." One supporting sentence.
3. **How it works** — three steps with small icons: ① Share to Trip Pocket from Instagram/TikTok ② Tag as place, food, or activity ③ Open the trip when you arrive.
4. **Features** — 4–6 cards: Share-sheet import · Auto text capture (on-device OCR) · Trip collections · Place / food / activity tags · Works offline · Private by default (nothing leaves your phone).
5. **Closing CTA** — repeat App Store badge, plus @trippocket.app link.
6. **Footer** — copyright, Privacy, Contact, Instagram @trippocket.app.

**Visual direction**
- Style: minimalist, modern, generous whitespace, soft shadows. Think Linear / Things 3 / Arc landing pages — not a typical "travel" site (no stock beach photos, no globe icons).
- Palette: warm off-white background (#FAF8F5), deep ink text (#1A1A1A), one accent color — a muted travel-passport teal or terracotta (pick one).
- Type: a clean modern sans (Inter, Geist, or similar). Large, confident hero headline. Tight, readable body.
- Imagery: real-feeling iPhone mockups over flat backgrounds. No gradients-on-gradients, no glassmorphism, no emoji decoration.
- Tone: calm and matter-of-fact. No marketing fluff, no "revolutionize," no exclamation marks.

**Constraints**
- Single page, responsive, mobile-first.
- No login, no email capture — App Store badge is the only conversion.
- Keep the whole page scannable in under 20 seconds.
